Free Checkups :

• Regular health checkups are crucial for early detection and prevention of diseases. Our hospital organizes free health checkup camps periodically in the community.

• These checkups cover a range of basic health parameters including blood pressure, blood sugar levels, cholesterol levels, and BMI (Body Mass Index).

• Qualified healthcare professionals conduct these checkups and provide personalized health advice and guidance based on the results. By offering free checkups, we aim to promote preventive healthcare and empower individuals to take proactive steps towards better health.
