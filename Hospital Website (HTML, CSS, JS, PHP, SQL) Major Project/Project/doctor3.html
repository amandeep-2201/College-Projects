Dr. Sunil Reddy

• Specialization : Neurology
• Education : Dr. Sunil Reddy completed her medical training at Columbia University College of Physicians and Surgeons. She then pursued residency training in Neurology at Massachusetts General Hospital, followed by a fellowship in Movement Disorders at Johns Hopkins Hospital.
• Experience : Dr. Reddy has a wealth of experience in the diagnosis and management of various neurological conditions, with a particular focus on movement disorders such as Parkinson's disease and essential tremor. She is skilled in performing deep brain stimulation surgeries for patients with advanced Parkinson's disease.
• Achievements : Dr. Reddy has received awards for her research in movement disorders and has published extensively in peer-reviewed journals. She is actively involved in clinical trials investigating new treatments for neurological disorders.
