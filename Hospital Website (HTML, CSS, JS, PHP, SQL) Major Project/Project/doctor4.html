Dr. Nisha Gupta

• Specialization : Cardiothoracic Surgery
• Education : Dr. Nisha Gupta earned his medical degree from Yale University School of Medicine. He completed his residency in General Surgery at Johns Hopkins Hospital, followed by a fellowship in Cardiothoracic Surgery at Cleveland Clinic.
• Experience : Dr. Gupta is a highly skilled cardiothoracic surgeon with expertise in complex cardiac and thoracic procedures. He has performed numerous coronary artery bypass surgeries, valve replacements, and lung resections, utilizing both traditional and minimally invasive techniques.
• Achievements : Dr. Gupta has been recognized for his surgical excellence and patient outcomes in cardiothoracic surgery. He has received awards from professional organizations and is involved in training the next generation of cardiothoracic surgeons.
