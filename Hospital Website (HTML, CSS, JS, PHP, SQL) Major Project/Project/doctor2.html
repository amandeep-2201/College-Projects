Dr. Harcharan Singh

• Specialization : Orthopedic Surgery
• Education : Dr. Harcharan Singh obtained his medical degree from Stanford University School of Medicine. He completed his residency in Orthopedic Surgery at Mayo Clinic and went on to pursue a fellowship in Sports Medicine at Hospital for Special Surgery in New York City.
• Experience : Dr. Singh has been practicing orthopedic surgery for over a decade, specializing in sports-related injuries and arthroscopic procedures. He has served as team physician for various professional sports teams and has a reputation for his innovative surgical techniques.
• Achievements : Dr. Singh has been recognized for his contributions to the field of orthopedic surgery, receiving awards for his research and patient care. He is actively involved in teaching and has trained many orthopedic surgeons.
