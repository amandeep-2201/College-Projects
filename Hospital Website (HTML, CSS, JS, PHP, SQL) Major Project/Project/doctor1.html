Dr. Vikram Singh Rathore

• Specialization : Pediatric Cardiology
• Education : Dr. Vikram Singh Rathore completed her medical degree at Harvard Medical School, followed by a residency in Pediatrics at Johns Hopkins Hospital. She then pursued further training with a fellowship in Pediatric Cardiology at Children's Hospital of Philadelphia.
• Experience : With over 15 years of experience in pediatric cardiology, Dr. Rathore has worked at several renowned children's hospitals across the country. She has a particular interest in congenital heart defects and has published numerous research papers on the subject.
• Achievements : Dr. Rathore has received several awards for her contributions to pediatric cardiology research and patient care. She is also actively involved in medical education and mentoring young physicians.
